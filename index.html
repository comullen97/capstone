Who's behind the app?!


Hi, my name is Casey! I have been working on creating a safe space for individuals like me who suffer from ADHD, anxiety & depression. I created this page a tool to help stay on track, and accomplish all those little tasks throughout the day. I have always suffered from getting overwhelmed easily, that would lead to procrastinating on tasks. This would lead to a snowball effect of being overwhelmed by house chores pilling up, and school, work, and other responsibilities not getting accomplished either. Don't worry you are not alone!

The Goal!


We all know the struggles mental illness can have on a persons daily life. And when someone is stuck in a depressive state of mind. Cleaning/hygiene is probably the last thing on your mind. However it is the easiest way to get out of the blues. So my goal is to help motivate, track and remind you of what to get done daily, weekly, and monthly! It is shown in studies that working or living in a clutter and dirty area increases levels or cortisol which is directly linked to anxiety, and even little acts of self care can have a large impact on how you manage stress, and increase your energy! Disclosure: this is NOT a cure for your mental health issues, but potential coping technics that can help improve your day!
